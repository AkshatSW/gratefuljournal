What are self-fulfilling prophecies?

A self-fulfilling prophecy is a belief or expectation that an individual holds about a future event that manifests because the individual holds it. Our mind is a powerful tool and we can use it to further ourselves or inhibit ourselves. If we believe that something will go wrong, our beliefs and expectations influence our behaviour at the subconscious level to ensure that our prophecy comes true.

Limiting beliefs gives rise to restrictive and negative thoughts which create self-fulfilling prophecies (often negative). We then act, behave, perceive, make choices and pay attention to things in accordance with our self-fulfilling prophecies. Undoubtedly what follows are the undesirable results of the prophecy, which further strengthens the limiting beliefs and perpetuates behaviour and thought patterns within us. This traps us in an endless and vicious cycle of self-sabotage, unhappy and unfulfilled life.

What is our inner child and how to take care of it?

The inner child is a part of our subconscious mind. It's that part of us which existed right from the moment we are born. It holds emotions, memories, and beliefs from the past as well as hopes and dreams for the future. When we make space for inner child healing, we often notice an overall improvement in understanding the self.

Tips to apply in your day-to-day life:

  1. 1. Building a relationship with the inner child by talking to it, and listening to what it has to say
  2. 2. Looking at old pictures of yourself and saying everything that you would have liked to hear when you were at that age.
  3. 3. Imagine that you are hugging your inner child.
  4. 4. Write a letter to your inner child.

Affirmations:

  1. 1. I love you
  2. 2. I'm sorry, you went through that, you did your best.
  3. 3. It's okay to love myself now as I continue to evolve.
