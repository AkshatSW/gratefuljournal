7 Chakras and their psychological functions

Chakra (chakra in Sanskrit) means “wheel” and refers to energy points in your body. They are thought to be spinning disks of energy that should stay “open” and aligned, as they correspond to bundles of nerves, major organs, and areas of our energetic body that affect our emotional and physical well-being. The 7 chakras or energy centres not only control and energize the internal organs but also control and affect one’s psychological conditions. They have corresponding psychological functions. Recognising the functions can help us manage our emotional well-being on a day to day life.

1. Root chakra (First chakra I Muladhara chakra)

Psychological Functioning: An enthusiasm is experienced as a result of the expanded consciousness at the root chakra. Enthusiasm is the foundation of all life and nurtures and enlivens our lives. Without enthusiasm, life will lose its meaning and become boring and stagnant.

The element: Earth

The location: Base of spine; Coccygeal Plexus

The healing practices: Reconnect with the body, Physical Activity, Touch, Massage, Bioenergetic grounding, Hath Yoga, Look at the earliest childhood relationship with the mother, and Reclaim the right to be here.

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. It is safe for me to be here
  2. 2. The earth supports me and meets my needs
  3. 3. I love my body and trust its wisdom
  4. 4. I am immersed in abundance
  5. 5. I'm here and I'm real

2. Sacral chakra (Second chakra I Swadhisthana chakra)

Psychological Functioning: The manifestations of upward-flowing energy are creativity, procreation, and sex drive. You could experience a lack of inspiration and creativity when the energy is moving downward.

The element: Water

The location: Sacral Plexus; Lower Abdomen

The healing practices: RMovement therapy, Emotional release or containment as appropriate, Inner child work, Boundary work, A 12-step program for addiction, Assigning healthy pleasure, and Develop Sensate intelligence.

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. I deserve pleasure in my life
  2. 2. I absorb information from my feelings
  3. 3. I embrace and celebrate my sexuality
  4. 4. My sexuality is sacred
  5. 5. I move easily and effortlessly
  6. 6. Life is pleasurable

3. Solar plexus chakra (Third chakra | Manipura chakra)

Psychological Functioning: TThis chakra's upward energy flow emerges as jealousy, greed, joy, and generosity

The element: Fire

The location: Solar Plexus

The healing practices: Risk-taking (deficiency), Grounding and emotional contact, Deep relaxation, stress control (excess)Vigorous exercise (running, aerobics, etc.), Martial arts, Sit-ups, Psychotherapy; Build Ego strength; release or contain anger; work on shame issues; strengthen the will, Encourage autonomy

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. I honour the power within me.
  2. 2. I accomplish tasks easily and effortlessly.
  3. 3. The fire within me burns through all blocks and fears.
  4. 4. I can do whatever I wish to do.

4. Heart chakra (Fourth chakra | Anahata chakra)

Psychological Functioning: Fear, love, and hatred are the three manifestations of the energy that flows through the heart chakra. Anyone who experiences love, hatred, or fear can feel it in their heart region.

The element: Air

The location: Chest, Heart, Cardiac Plexus

The healing practices: Breathing exercises, Pranayama, Work with arms, reaching out, taking in, Journaling, Self Discovery, Psychotherapy, Examining assumptions about relationships, Emotional release of grief, Forgiveness with appropriate inner child work, Codependency work, Self-acceptance, Anima-Animus integration.

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. I am worthy of love
  2. 2. I am loving to myself and others
  3. 3. There is an infinite supply of love
  4. 4. I live in balance with others

5. Throat chakra (Fifth chakra | Vishuddhi chakra)

Psychological Functioning: Gratitude and grief are the manifestations of the energy when it moves through the throat chakra. When you are grieving, your throat gets choked. Your throat also gets choked up when you're feeling especially grateful.

The element: Sound

The location: Throat, Pharyngeal plexus

The healing practices: Loosen neck and shoulders, Release voice, Singing, Chanting, Toning, Storytelling, Journal writing, Automatic writing, Practice silence (excess), Nongoal-oriented creativity, Psychotherapy, Learning communication skills, Complete communication, Letter writing, Inner child communication, Voice dialogue

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. I hear and speak the truth
  2. 2. I express myself with clear intent
  3. 3. Creativity flows in and through me
  4. 4. My voice is necessary

6. Third eye chakra (Sixth chakra | Ajna chakra)

Psychological Functioning: Anger, Awareness and Alertness are the three forms in which energy manifests when it flows through this chakra. The third eye chakra is the seat of wisdom and knowledge that has been awakened, as well as the seat of anger

The element: Light

The location: Forehead, brow, Carotid plexus, Third Eye

The healing practices: Create visual art, Visual stimulation, Meditation, Psychotherapy, Colouring and drawing, Art therapy, Working with memory, Connecting images with feeling, Dreamwork, Hypnosis, Guided visualization, and Past life regression therapy

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. I see things in clarity
  2. 2. I am open to the wisdom within
  3. 3. I can manifest my vision

7. Crown chakra (Seventh chakra | Sahasrara chakra)

Psychological Functioning: When energy rises to the top of the head, one experiences joy. Duality, conflict, and separation disappear once someone is firmly established there. You feel connected and one with everything.

The element: Thought

The location: Cerebral Cortex

TReestablish physical, and emotional connection (excess), Reestablish spirit communication, Learning and study, Spiritual discipline, Meditation, Psychotherapy, Examine belief systems, Develop inner witness, and Work with a higher powe

    Affirmations:

  1. 1. Divinity resides within
  2. 2. I am open to new ideas
  3. 3. Information I need comes to me
  4. 4. The world is my teacher
  5. 5. I am guided by the higher power
  6. 6. I am guided by inner wisdom
